2️⃣ Get RSS Feeds for YouTube, Twitter, and Spotify

- YouTube RSS Feed (Works Natively)
    Replace CHANNEL_ID with the actual YouTube channel ID:
    https://www.youtube.com/feeds/videos.xml?channel_id=CHANNEL_ID

- Twitter RSS Feed (Use Nitter)
    Nitter is a Twitter alternative that generates RSS feeds:
    https://nitter.net/TWITTER_USERNAME/rss

- Spotify RSS Feed (For Podcasts)
    Some Spotify podcasts have public RSS feeds.
    You can find them in the Spotify for Podcasters settings.
